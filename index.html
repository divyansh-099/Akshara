Dear Akshara

Dear Akshara, ❤️ There are some feelings that become impossible to hide, no matter how hard you try. From the moment you became a part of my life, the ordinary started feeling extraordinary. Somehow, your smile, your voice, and even the smallest conversations with you stayed with me long after they ended. I don't know what tomorrow holds, and I don't expect you to have an answer immediately. I just wanted to be honest with you. I love you. Not because you're perfect, but because being around you makes life feel a little brighter, a little happier, and a lot more meaningful. If you'll let me... I'd love to stand beside you, make countless memories together, laugh at silly things, support each other through difficult days, and see where this beautiful journey takes us. So here's the question my heart has wanted to ask for a long time... Will you be mine forever? ❤️ ❤️ — Divyansh
